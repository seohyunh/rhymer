Rhyme-R

Instructions

  • Try to enter as many rhymes as you can in one minute!
  • The word you're rhyming is randomly generated
  • The countdown starts as soon as the word is generated
  • if you're struggling, try to think of alternate pronounciations
  • Use the "enter" key to submit your rhymes

About
